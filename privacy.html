Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 24, 2025

BodhPrepIAS ("we", "our", "us") provides tools to plan and track focus sessions. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Account data: If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google user ID, email, name, and avatar URL. We do not receive your password.
  • Usage data: Session names, durations, timers, and actions you take in the app.
  • Device/technical data: Basic logs (IP, browser type, pages viewed) for security and reliability.
  • Cookies and tokens: We use short-lived access tokens and optional refresh tokens to keep you signed in. Cookies used for auth are httpOnly and secure.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve BodhPrepIAS's features (e.g., saving sessions, timers).
  • Authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • Prevent abuse and ensure reliable service.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Legal Bases

  • Performance of a contract: To deliver the app’s features you request.
  • Legitimate interests: To keep the service secure and improve usability.
  • Consent: Where required (e.g., analytics cookies if enabled).

Data Sharing

  • Authentication providers: Google is used to sign you in; Google receives standard OAuth data during sign-in.
  • Service providers: We may use hosting, logging, and analytics services. They are bound by contractual obligations.
  • Legal: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and integrity of the service.

Data Retention

We retain account and session data for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion, we remove or anonymize your data within a reasonable period, subject to legal requirements.

Security

We use industry-standard measures such as HTTPS, least-privilege access, hashed refresh tokens, and regular updates. No method is 100% secure; we work continuously to improve protections.

Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your information.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Port your data (export sessions).
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.

To exercise rights, contact us using the details below.

Children’s Privacy

BodhPrepIAS is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us to delete it.

International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own. We use safeguards such as contractual clauses to protect your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in practices or legal requirements. We will update the effective date and, where material, provide reasonable notice.
